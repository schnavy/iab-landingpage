Independent Publishing Fair Leipzig, March 19, 2022. It’s a book, it’s a word that you choose, it’s a language in use. Opening hours 12 pm – 9 pm, symposium 1 pm – 6 pm. Please be welcome to visit our marketplace and symposium at the Academy of Fine Arts Leipzig, Wächterstraße 11, 04107 Leipzig. Join the digital event right here! (www.itsabook.de). Submit your books until February 14. Publishers to publishers@itsabook.de and students using this application form to students@itsabook.de. Our on-site event will take place as planned.
